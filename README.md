# e-piggy-bank
My webpage is not normal. It’s a masterpiece made by me 
But how exactly is it a masterpiece?
Well, I made it specially for students, and also, no this not a real bank
It is just a webpage which just teaches students how to deposit, withdraw, create an account and check their balance.
When you click on my website, you will find it vibrant in color. As you open it you will have to create an account to continue.
Next thing you want to do is add your password and name if u don’t then it will show an alert.
So whenever you want to add money to your account, feel free to! But first you need to add a valid amount or you will see a alert.
If you want to check your balance- just click on it!
